About Xenoplay TV

Xenoplay TV is a modern streaming application built for users who want fast access to live cricket, sports coverage, and entertainment channels directly on Android devices. It combines live matches, TV channels, and multiple categories in one smooth and easy-to-use platform.

Beyond sports, Xenoplay TV is also popular among users who enjoy dramas, serials, and movies. With its lightweight design and optimized performance, the app delivers a clean and stable experience even on mid-range phones.
